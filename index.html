Types of Water Sources

Fresh water comes in two forms: safe and risky. Safe options, like piped water or protected wells, are improved sources. These shield water from contamination. Unimproved sources, like unprotected springs or rivers, can be polluted. Rainwater collection and bottled water are also considered improved sources, while rivers and lakes often fall under unimproved due to potential contamination. Access to improved water is vital for health, reducing waterborne diseases.
